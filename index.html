About

This is the version of me that refuses to stay still.

I am Sergio Fernandes, but here I move as शस्फ: camera in hand, Goa in the background, school-corridor noise still somewhere in my head, and a habit of turning normal walks into tiny films.

I like footage that feels a little unplanned. A face too close to the lens. A road with no grand reason. Rain on a quiet corner. A funny thought that suddenly becomes honest.
